## 一、核心句式体系

### 1. Can you show me...? / Could you explain...?

**请求与询问**

- Can you show me the latest design mockups?
- Could you explain the design concept?
- Can you walk me through the user flow?
- Could you share the design specifications?
- Can you show me how this interaction works?
- Could you elaborate on the color choice?
- Can you demonstrate the prototype?
- Could you clarify the layout structure?

---

### 2. I'm thinking of... / My idea is...

**表达设计想法**

- I'm thinking of a minimalist approach.
- My idea is to use a card-based layout.
- I was considering a darker color palette.
- My concept is to focus on accessibility.
- I believe a modular design would work better.
- I'm leaning towards a clean and simple aesthetic.

---

### 3. What do you think about...? / How do you feel about...?

**征求反馈**

- What do you think about this typography?
- How do you feel about the spacing?
- What are your thoughts on the color scheme?
- How do you like the overall composition?
- What do you think of the iconography?
- How does the hierarchy feel to you?

---

### 4. I recommend... / I'd suggest...

**提出建议**

- I recommend increasing the button size.
- I'd suggest simplifying the navigation.
- I recommend using a consistent grid system.
- I'd suggest adding more white space.
- I recommend conducting user testing.
- I'd suggest reviewing the contrast ratios.

---

### 5. The design conveys... / This layout communicates...

**设计表达**

- The design conveys a professional image.
- This layout communicates clarity and focus.
- The color palette conveys trust and reliability.
- The typography communicates modernity.
- The visual hierarchy guides the user's eye.
- This animation conveys playfulness.

---

### 6. Let me share... / I'd like to present...

**展示设计**

- Let me share my screen.
- I'd like to present the new design direction.
- Let me walk you through the key screens.
- I'd like to show you the updated mockups.
- Let me share the design system documentation.
- I'd like to demonstrate the prototype.

---

### 7. I'm sorry... / I apologize for...

**道歉表达**

- I'm sorry, I misunderstood the requirements.
- I apologize for the delay in sending the files.
- I'm sorry for the confusion about the timeline.
- I apologize for not including you in the review.
- I'm sorry, this isn't what you described.
- I apologize for the oversight in the design.

---

### 8. Thank you for... / I appreciate...

**感谢表达**

- Thank you for your feedback.
- I appreciate your input on the design.
- Thank you for the clear requirements.
- I appreciate your patience with revisions.
- Thank you for sharing your thoughts.
- I appreciate the quick turnaround.

---

### 9. I agree... / I disagree...

**表达同意/不同意**

- I agree, the navigation needs improvement.
- I agree, this is a strong concept.
- I disagree, I think the contrast is sufficient.
- I see your point, but I have a different perspective.
- I partially agree with your feedback.
- I'd like to discuss this further.

---

### 10. Let me clarify... / To clarify...

**澄清说明**

- Let me clarify the design requirements.
- To clarify, the primary user is...
- Let me clarify the scope of this project.
- To clarify, the deliverables include...
- Let me make sure we're on the same page.

## 二、设计概念英语

### 2.1 UI设计原则

**一致性：**
- Consistency is key throughout the interface.
- We should maintain visual consistency across all screens.
- The design should follow established patterns.
- Inconsistent design creates user confusion.
- Let's align with the existing design system.

**层次结构：**
- Visual hierarchy helps users navigate.
- The primary action should be most prominent.
- Secondary information should be clearly subordinate.
- Typography scale creates natural hierarchy.
- Color can emphasize important elements.

**对齐与间距：**
- Elements should be properly aligned.
- Consistent spacing improves readability.
- Grid systems ensure alignment.
- Padding should be uniform throughout.
- Let's use an 8px grid system.

**对比度：**
- Contrast affects accessibility.
- Text must meet WCAG contrast requirements.
- Low contrast makes content hard to read.
- We need to test contrast ratios.
- Consider contrast for interactive elements.

---

### 2.2 颜色与排版

**颜色讨论：**
- What color palette are you considering?
- The primary color should be brand-consistent.
- Neutral colors work well for backgrounds.
- Accent colors draw attention to CTAs.
- Consider color blindness accessibility.
- The color mood should match the brand.

**排版讨论：**
- What typeface would fit the brand?
- We should use a maximum of two typefaces.
- Font weights create visual hierarchy.
- Line height affects readability.
- The type scale should be consistent.
- Consider mobile typography needs.

---

### 2.3 布局与结构

**页面结构：**
- The header should contain navigation.
- A sidebar provides easy access to features.
- Content should be organized in logical sections.
- Footer typically contains links and copyright.
- Card layouts work well for content grouping.
- Grid layouts ensure consistent alignment.

**响应式设计：**
- The design needs to be responsive.
- Mobile-first approach is recommended.
- Breakpoints should be defined for each device.
- Content reflows appropriately on different screens.
- Touch targets need to be large enough.
- Consider landscape and portrait orientations.

---

### 2.4 组件与元素

**按钮：**
- Primary buttons should be most prominent.
- Secondary buttons are less visually dominant.
- Button states include default, hover, and active.
- Disabled states should be clearly indicated.
- Consider button sizing for touch.

**表单：**
- Form labels should be clearly visible.
- Input fields need proper placeholder text.
- Error messages should be helpful.
- Required fields should be indicated.
- Consider inline validation.
- Group related form elements together.

**图标：**
- Icons should be consistent in style.
- Use recognizable symbols for common actions.
- Icon sizing should follow guidelines.
- Consider icon accessibility.
- Outline and filled styles should be consistent.
- Test icons at different sizes.

---

### 2.5 动效与交互

**动画讨论：**
- Animations should serve a purpose.
- Micro-interactions enhance user experience.
- Transitions should be smooth but quick.
- Avoid distracting or excessive animation.
- Consider reduced motion preferences.
- Timing functions affect animation feel.

**交互设计：**
- Hover states provide feedback.
- Loading states manage user expectations.
- Transitions guide users between states.
- Gestures should be intuitive.
- Consider swipe actions for mobile.
- Feedback confirms user actions.

## 三、设计评审英语

### 3.1 评审开场

**主持评审：**
- Thank you all for joining today's design review.
- Let's look at the latest designs for the dashboard.
- I'll walk you through the key screens.
- We're here to gather feedback on the direction.
- Please share your thoughts openly.

**介绍设计：**
- This is the main user dashboard.
- The design follows our established system.
- I've focused on improving the user flow.
- We wanted to address the accessibility concerns.
- The new design prioritizes mobile users.

---

### 3.2 收集反馈

**询问意见：**
- What are your first impressions?
- Does this meet the user needs?
- Are there any concerns about the layout?
- How does this align with the brand?
- Does the interaction feel intuitive?
- Any thoughts on the visual hierarchy?

**给予反馈：**
- I think the navigation is clear.
- The color usage is consistent with our brand.
- The typography is easy to read.
- The spacing creates good visual rhythm.
- I have concerns about the loading time.
- The hierarchy could be stronger.

---

### 3.3 设计辩护

**解释设计决策：**
- I chose this approach because...
- This aligns with user research findings.
- The decision was based on accessibility standards.
- We prioritized based on user feedback.
- This follows industry best practices.
- The trade-off was intentional.

**回应批评：**
- That's a valid point.
- I understand your concern.
- Let me explain the reasoning.
- We can explore that in the next iteration.
- I appreciate that perspective.
- How about we compromise on...?

---

### 3.4 记录与跟进

**记录反馈：**
- Let me jot down these points.
- I'll create a list of action items.
- Let me summarize the key takeaways.
- I'll share the notes after the meeting.
- We should prioritize these changes.

**跟进表达：**
- I'll incorporate this feedback.
- Let me revise and share an update.
- I'll address the concerns in the next version.
- Let me schedule a follow-up review.
- I'll research solutions for the issues raised.

## 四、与开发协作英语

### 4.1 设计交接

**交接说明：**
- Here are the design files for development.
- I've organized assets in the shared folder.
- The specs include all necessary measurements.
- I've documented the interaction details.
- Please refer to the style guide.
- Let me know if you have any questions.

**标注说明：**
- All measurements are in pixels.
- I've marked the safe areas for notches.
- Color codes are in the spec sheet.
- Typography styles are defined in the system.
- Spacing follows the 8px grid.
- Interactive states are documented.

---

### 4.2 解答疑问

**开发提问：**
- Can you clarify the spacing here?
- What should happen on this hover state?
- Is this animation implemented in CSS or Lottie?
- Can you confirm the breakpoint for this layout?
- How should we handle overflow text?
- What's the priority for implementing these states?

**解答表达：**
- The spacing should be 16px on mobile.
- Hover states should use a subtle highlight.
- This animation should be done with CSS.
- The breakpoint is at 768px.
- Overflow text should use ellipsis.
- Let's prioritize the default and hover states.

---

### 4.3 解决问题

**讨论实现：**
- This might be challenging to implement.
- Let's find a simpler approach.
- We could use a workaround for now.
- The design intent is important here.
- Can we adjust the design for easier implementation?
- Let's discuss the technical constraints.

**协作表达：**
- I'll create a simplified version.
- Let's pair on this implementation.
- Can you try a different approach?
- I'll update the spec to reflect reality.
- Let's test how it looks in code.
- We can iterate on this in the next sprint.

---

### 4.4 设计系统

**讨论组件：**
- Let's use components from the design system.
- We should create reusable components.
- The button styles need to be consistent.
- Can we add this to the component library?
- The system needs to be updated.
- Let's document this pattern.

**规范维护：**
- The design system is the source of truth.
- We should follow the existing patterns.
- Any new additions should be documented.
- Let's review the system periodically.
- The system should evolve with our needs.
- Accessibility is built into the system.

## 五、客户与产品沟通

### 5.1 理解需求

**需求询问：**
- Can you describe your target audience?
- What problem are we solving for users?
- What are the key features required?
- Who will be using this product?
- What devices do users primarily use?
- What is the expected timeline?

**确认理解：**
- Let me confirm my understanding.
- So, the main goal is to...
- You're looking for a design that...
- The primary users are...
- The core functionality includes...
- Is my understanding correct?

---

### 5.2 展示方案

**演示设计：**
- Let me walk you through the proposed design.
- I've created several design directions.
- This mockup shows the main user flow.
- The design is mobile-responsive.
- I've considered the accessibility requirements.

**解释价值：**
- This design will improve user engagement.
- The clear hierarchy helps users navigate.
- Consistent styling builds brand trust.
- The simplified flow reduces friction.
- Accessibility ensures everyone can use it.

---

### 5.3 管理期望

**处理异议：**
- I understand you want more features.
- Let's prioritize the must-haves first.
- We can add more in future iterations.
- The current scope is ambitious.
- What if we phase the development?
- Let's focus on the core experience first.

**协商表达：**
- I recommend we focus on quality over quantity.
- We can explore other options within budget.
- The timeline affects what's possible.
- Let's find a creative solution.
- I suggest a compromise.
- How about we prioritize differently?

---

### 5.4 项目管理

**进度更新：**
- The design phase is on track.
- We're currently in the wireframing stage.
- High-fidelity mockups will be ready next week.
- The prototype is 80% complete.
- We're awaiting feedback to proceed.

**时间讨论：**
- Can we extend the timeline?
- The design requires more iterations.
- We need additional resources for this.
- The scope has expanded significantly.
- Can we prioritize certain features?
- Let's adjust the milestones.
