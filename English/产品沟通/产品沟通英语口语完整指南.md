## 一、核心句式体系

### 1. I'd like to... / I want to...

**表达愿望**

- I'd like to discuss the product roadmap.
- I want to propose a new feature.
- I'd like to gather requirements.
- I want to understand the user needs.
- I'd like to schedule a product review.
- I want to prioritize this feature.

---

### 2. Can you tell me about...? / Could you explain...?

**询问需求**

- Can you tell me about the user pain points?
- Could you explain the business requirements?
- Can you tell me about the user journey?
- Could you explain why this is important?
- Can you tell me about the success metrics?
- Could you explain the user feedback?

---

### 3. The goal is to... / Our objective is to...

**目标表达**

- The goal is to improve user retention.
- Our objective is to increase conversion.
- The goal is to reduce churn.
- Our objective is to enhance user experience.
- The goal is to launch on time.
- Our objective is to meet user needs.

---

### 4. Let me share... / I'd like to present...

**展示与分享**

- Let me share the product vision.
- I'd like to present the roadmap.
- Let me walk you through the requirements.
- I'd like to show you the user research.
- Let me share the metrics dashboard.
- I'd like to demonstrate the prototype.

---

### 5. What do you think about...? / How do you feel about...?

**征求反馈**

- What do you think about this approach?
- How do you feel about the timeline?
- What do you think of the feature priority?
- How do you feel about the design?
- What do you think about the user flow?
- How do you feel about the scope?

---

### 6. I recommend... / I'd suggest...

**建议表达**

- I recommend we prioritize this feature.
- I'd suggest we conduct user research first.
- I recommend launching in phases.
- I'd suggest reducing the scope.
- I recommend a MVP approach.
- I'd suggest more user testing.

---

### 7. The challenge is... / The issue is...

**挑战与问题**

- The challenge is the tight timeline.
- The issue is limited resources.
- The challenge is technical complexity.
- The issue is unclear requirements.
- The challenge is stakeholder alignment.
- The issue is competing priorities.

---

### 8. Let me clarify... / To clarify...

**澄清说明**

- Let me clarify the requirements.
- To clarify, the scope includes...
- Let me clarify the success criteria.
- To clarify, the timeline is...
- Let me clarify the user's needs.
- To clarify, the priorities are...

---

### 9. I'm sorry... / I apologize for...

**道歉表达**

- I'm sorry for the confusion.
- I apologize for the delayed response.
- I'm sorry for the scope change.
- I apologize for not updating you sooner.
- I'm sorry, I misunderstood your requirement.
- I apologize for the oversight.

---

### 10. Thank you for... / I appreciate...

**感谢表达**

- Thank you for your feedback.
- I appreciate your input.
- Thank you for the clarification.
- I appreciate your patience.
- Thank you for your support.
- I appreciate the team's effort.

---

## 二、需求管理英语

### 2.1 需求收集

**询问需求：**
- Can you describe the feature you need?
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who are the target users?
- What is the expected outcome?
- What constraints should we consider?
- What is the business impact?

**理解需求：**
- Let me make sure I understand.
- So, you need the ability to...
- The core requirement is to...
- Is my understanding correct?
- Let me summarize the requirements.
- The key needs are...

---

### 2.2 需求文档

**PRD讨论：**
- I've drafted the PRD.
- The product requirements document is ready.
- Let's review the acceptance criteria.
- The requirements are clearly defined.
- Can you review the PRD?
- We need stakeholder sign-off.

**功能描述：**
- The feature should allow users to...
- Users need the ability to...
- The system must support...
- As a user, I want to...
- The feature enables...
- Success looks like...

---

### 2.3 用户故事

**用户故事表达：**
- As a user, I can...
- As an admin, I want to...
- So that I can achieve...
- The acceptance criteria are...
- This story has these conditions...
- The story is estimated at...

**讨论故事：**
- Let's break down the epics.
- How should we prioritize this?
- The story depends on...
- This story is too large.
- Let's split this user story.
- The story is ready for development.

---

### 2.4 优先级排序

**优先级讨论：**
- We need to prioritize features.
- This should be P0 priority.
- Let's use MoSCoW prioritization.
- This is a must-have feature.
- This is nice-to-have.
- Can we defer this to later?

**排序依据：**
- Based on user impact...
- Considering business value...
- Based on development effort...
- The ROI justifies priority.
- User research supports this.
- Technical dependencies matter.

---

### 2.5 需求变更

**变更管理：**
- We have a change request.
- The requirements have evolved.
- Can we discuss the scope change?
- This impacts the timeline.
- Let's assess the impact.
- Change request needs approval.

**处理变更：**
- I understand the need for change.
- Let's evaluate the trade-offs.
- Can we add this to the backlog?
- We need to reprioritize.
- This will require additional resources.
- Let's find a creative solution.

- Let's track milestone progress.

---

### 3.2 敏捷管理

**Sprint讨论：**
- Let's plan the sprint.
- What's in the sprint backlog?
- We have 2 weeks for this sprint.
- The sprint goal is...
- Can we fit this in the sprint?
- Sprint velocity is stable.

**Daily Standup表达：**
- Yesterday I worked on...
- Today I'll focus on...
- I'm blocked on...
- The feature is almost complete.
- I need help with...
- No blockers from my side.

**Sprint Review：**
- Here's what we accomplished.
- The sprint goal was achieved.
- We completed 8 user stories.
- Let's demo the features.
- Feedback is welcome.
- What should we improve?

**Retrospective：**
- What went well?
- What could be improved?
- Let's discuss the blockers.
- Action items for next sprint.
- Team synergy is strong.
- We need to address...

---

### 3.3 进度跟踪

**状态更新：**
- The project is on track.
- We're slightly behind schedule.
- Progress is being made.
- Milestone 1 is complete.
- Development is 70% done.
- Testing will begin next week.

**风险与问题：**

- The issue needs escalation.
- We need to address this blocker.
- Technical debt is accumulating.
- Resource constraints exist.
- Dependencies are the concern.

**汇报表达：**
- Here's the weekly status update.
- The project is progressing well.
- We're facing some challenges.
- Mitigation plans are in place.
- Stakeholders are updated regularly.
- Executive summary is attached.

## 四、数据分析与指标

### 4.1 产品指标

**关键指标：**
- DAU shows user engagement.
- MAU indicates broader reach.
- Retention rate measures loyalty.
- Conversion rate is low.
- Churn rate needs to improve.
- NPS reflects user satisfaction.

**指标讨论：**
- What metrics should we track?
- Let's review the analytics.
- The data shows a trend.
- User behavior is changing.
- We need more data points.
- The numbers tell a story.

---

### 4.2 A/B测试

**测试讨论：**
- Let's run an A/B test.
- We're testing two variants.
- Sample size needs to be sufficient.
- Statistical significance matters.
- Results will guide decision.
- Test duration is 2 weeks.

**结果分析：**
- Variant B outperforms A.
- The test is inconclusive.
- We have statistical confidence.
- Let's implement the winner.
- Further testing is needed.
- The results inform our direction.

---

### 4.3 增长分析

**增长讨论：**

- Viral coefficient is above 1.
- LTV is increasing.
- CAC needs to decrease.
- The funnel has drop-offs.
- Optimization opportunities exist.

**漏斗分析：**
- Users drop off at checkout.
- Activation rate is improving.
- The funnel needs optimization.
- Each step has friction.
- Let's simplify the flow.
- Friction points are identified.

## 五、用户研究与反馈

### 5.1 用户访谈

**访谈准备：**
- Let's schedule user interviews.
- Participant recruitment is underway.
- Discussion guide is prepared.
- Interview objectives are clear.
- Sessions will be recorded.
- Notes will be shared afterward.

**访谈进行：**
- Thank you for joining.
- Can you tell me about...?
- How do you currently handle...?
- Walk me through your process.
- What frustrates you about...?
- How would you improve...?

**访谈总结：**
- Key insights are documented.
- User needs are identified.
- Pain points are categorized.
- User quotes are illuminating.
- Recommendations are made.
- Research report is ready.

---

**反馈分析：**

- Criticism centers on...
- Feature requests are logged.

**用户细分：**

- Enterprise users have requirements.
- B2B vs B2C differs.
- User segments are distinct.

### 5.4 竞品分析

**SWOT分析：**

- Threat from new entrants.
- Competitive landscape is shifting.
- Market trends are analyzed.
