## 一、核心句式体系

### 1. Good morning/afternoon/evening

**问候表达**

- Good morning, everyone!
- Good afternoon, team.
- Good evening, everyone.
- Good morning! How are you?
- Good afternoon. How's your day going?
- Good evening. Hope you had a good day.

---

### 2. Can you...? / Could you...?

**请求帮助**

- Can you help me with this?
- Can you review my document?
- Can you send me the file?
- Can you join the meeting?
- Can you explain this to me?
- Could you take a look at this?
- Could you give me a hand?
- Could you spare a moment?

---

### 3. Thank you... / Thanks...

**感谢表达**

- Thank you for your help.
- Thanks for the information.
- Thank you for your time.
- Thanks for the quick response.
- Thank you for understanding.
- Thanks for being patient.
- Thank you so much!
- Thanks a million!

---

### 4. I'm sorry... / I apologize...

**道歉表达**

- I'm sorry for the delay.
- I'm sorry for the inconvenience.
- I apologize for the confusion.
- I'm sorry, I didn't mean to.
- I apologize for missing the meeting.
- I'm sorry if I offended you.
- My apologies for the oversight.

---

### 5. I think... / In my opinion...

**表达观点**

- I think we should proceed.
- In my opinion, this is the best approach.
- I think there might be a better way.
- In my view, we need more time.
- I believe this will work.
- From my perspective, this makes sense.

---

### 6. Let me... / I'll...

**提出行动**

- Let me know if you need anything.
- Let me check on that.
- Let me get back to you.
- Let me share my screen.
- Let me think about it.
- I'll send you the details.
- I'll follow up on this.
- I'll take care of it.

---

### 7. Do you mind if...? / Would you mind...?

**询问许可**

- Do you mind if I join the call?
- Do you mind if I close the window?
- Would you mind sharing the document?
- Would you mind explaining that again?
- Do you mind me asking a question?
- Would you mind if I borrowed this?

---

### 8. I appreciate... / I value...

**表达感激**

- I appreciate your hard work.
- I appreciate your feedback.
- I value your input.
- I appreciate your patience.
- I value our collaboration.
- I appreciate you letting me know.

---

### 9. That sounds great! / That makes sense.

**回应表达**

- That sounds great!
- That makes sense.
- Sure, no problem!
- Absolutely!
- Of course!
- Go ahead!
- I'm with you on that.

---

### 10. Let me clarify... / To clarify...

**澄清说明**

- Let me clarify what I mean.
- To clarify, we're meeting at 3pm.
- Let me make sure I understand.
- Just to clarify, the deadline is Friday.
- Let me be more specific.
- To be clear, this is urgent.

---

## 二、会议交流英语

### 2.1 会议开场

**主持会议：**
- Good morning, everyone. Let's get started.
- Thank you all for joining.
- I think we're all here now.
- Shall we begin?
- Let's kick off the meeting.
- Welcome, everyone, to today's meeting.

**自我介绍：**
- Let me introduce myself briefly.
- For those who don't know me, I'm...
- I'm the project lead for this initiative.
- I'll be facilitating today's session.
I'll be chairing this meeting.

- I'm here to give an update on...

---

### 2.2 会议进行

**提出议题：**
- Let's move on to the first item.
- The agenda for today is...
- I'd like to discuss...
- Our topic today is...
- Let's start with...
- The purpose of this meeting is...

**表达意见：**
- I'd like to share my thoughts.
- In my experience...
- From my point of view...
- I'd suggest we...
- The way I see it...
- I have a different perspective.

**询问意见：**
- What do you think about this?
- Any thoughts on this?
- Does anyone have input?
- How does that sound to everyone?
- Are there any concerns?
- What are your views on this?

---

### 2.3 打断与插话

**礼貌打断：**
- Sorry to interrupt, but...
- May I add something here?
- If I could just interject...
- Could I just say something?
- Sorry, can I jump in here?
- I'd like to briefly add...

**回应打断：**
- Sure, go ahead.
- Please, continue.
- That's a good point.
- Thanks for the input.
- I was just getting to that.
- Let me finish my point first.

---

### 2.4 会议讨论

**同意观点：**
- I agree with that.
- That's a valid point.
- I couldn't agree more.
- Absolutely, I second that.
- I see eye to eye with you.
- That's exactly what I was thinking.

**不同意：**
- I see it differently.
- I'm not sure I agree.
- I have a different view.
- I understand, but...
- That might not work because...
- I'd have to respectfully disagree.

**澄清问题：**
- Can you elaborate on that?
- Could you give an example?
- What do you mean by...?
- I'm not quite following.
- Could you clarify that point?
- Can you explain further?

---

### 2.5 会议结束

**总结讨论：**
- Let me summarize what we've discussed.
- To summarize, our key takeaways are...
- In summary, we've agreed to...
- So, to wrap up...
- Let me recap the main points.

**分配任务：**
- John will take the lead on this.
- Sarah will follow up with the client.
- Let's assign action items now.
- I'll send out the meeting notes.
- Please complete your tasks by Friday.

**结束会议：**
- That covers everything on the agenda.
- Thank you all for attending.
- See you all next week.
- Have a great day, everyone!
- The meeting is adjourned.

---

### 2.6 视频会议

**技术问题：**
- Can you hear me now?
- You're on mute.
- Let me unmute myself.
- Could you turn on your camera?
- The connection is a bit choppy.
- Let me try reconnecting.
- You're breaking up a little.

**屏幕共享：**
- Let me share my screen.
- Can you see my screen?
- I'll stop sharing for now.
- Do you want me to share the document?
- The shared screen looks good.
- Let me know when you can see it.

## 三、邮件沟通英语

### 3.1 邮件开头

**正式问候：**
- Dear Mr. Smith,
- Dear Ms. Johnson,
- Dear Team,
- Hello everyone,
- Good morning/afternoon,

**询问开头：**
- I hope this email finds you well.
- I hope you're doing well.
- I'm writing to inquire about...
- I'm reaching out regarding...
- I'm following up on our conversation.
- I wanted to follow up with you.

---

### 3.2 邮件正文

**请求表达：**
- I would appreciate it if you could...
- Could you please send me...
- Would you mind sharing...
- I was wondering if you could...
- I would be grateful if you could...
- Please let me know if you need anything.

**信息传递：**
- I'm happy to inform you that...
- I wanted to update you on...
- As discussed, we'll...
- Just a quick note to remind you...
- Please be advised that...
- I wanted to bring to your attention...

**附件说明：**
- I've attached the document for your review.
- Please find the attached report.
- The attached file contains...
- I'm attaching the updated version.
- Let me know if you can't open the attachment.

---

### 3.3 邮件结尾

**期待回复：**
- Looking forward to hearing from you.
- Hope to hear from you soon.
- Please let me know your thoughts.
- I'd appreciate your feedback.
- Please get back to me at your earliest convenience.

**结束语：**
- Best regards,
- Kind regards,
- Best wishes,
- Sincerely,
- Thanks again,
- Cheers,

---

### 3.4 邮件主题

**常见主题：**
- Meeting Request: Project Update
- Follow-up: Q3 Planning Discussion
- Action Required: Budget Approval
- FYI: New Policy Implementation
- Urgent: Server Maintenance Tonight
- Quick Question About...
- Thank You for Your Help
- Re: Agenda for Tomorrow's Meeting

- How's your day going?
- How have you been?
- Good to see you!
- Long time no see!
- How's life treating you?

**回应问候：**

- Pretty good, how about you?
- Can't complain!
- Doing well, thank you for asking.
- All good here!
- Not bad, thanks for asking.
- Been busy lately!
- Just keeping busy!

---

### 4.2 介绍与相识

**介绍自己：**
- Let me introduce myself.
- I'm pleased to meet you.
- I've heard a lot about you.
- It's nice to put a face to the name.
- Welcome to the team!
- Feel free to reach out to me.

**介绍他人：**
- Have you met Sarah?
- I'd like you to meet John.
- This is our new team member.
- Sarah works in marketing.
- John is our tech lead.
- Let me connect you two.

**升职/成功：**

- That's wonderful news!

**节日祝福：**
- Happy holidays!
- Wishing you a wonderful holiday season.
- Merry Christmas!
- Happy New Year!
- Have a great weekend!

### 4.4 安慰与支持

**表达同情：**

- My thoughts are with you.
- If there's anything I can do, let me know.
- Take care of yourself.
- I hope things get better soon.

**鼓励表达：**
- You've got this!
- I believe in you.
- You're doing great work.
- Keep it up!
- Don't give up!
- I'm proud of you.

---

### 4.5 道别与祝福

- Have a wonderful evening!
- Take care!
- See you tomorrow!
- See you around!
- Talk to you soon!
- Have a safe trip!

- Leaving for the day!
- Heading out, see you tomorrow!
- Have a great evening, everyone!
- I'm off for today. Bye!

## 五、职场关系英语

### 5.1 与上司沟通

**汇报工作：**
- Here's my weekly update.
- I've completed the project.
- Just wanted to update you on...
- I wanted to give you a heads up.
- Could we schedule a one-on-one?
- Do you have time to discuss...?

**寻求指导：**
- I'd like your advice on...
- What are your thoughts on...?
- Could you help me prioritize...?
- Would you mind reviewing...?
- I'd appreciate your guidance.

**接受反馈：**
- Thank you for the feedback.
- I'll take that into account.
- I appreciate your perspective.
- That's helpful, thank you.
- I'll work on improving that.

---

### 5.2 与同事沟通

**协作表达：**
- I'm happy to collaborate on this.
- Let's work together on this.
- Can we pair on this task?
- I'll support you on this.
- We're in this together.
- Teamwork makes the dream work!

**请求配合：**
- Could you help me with...?
- I need your input on...
- Your expertise would be valuable.
- Would you mind reviewing...?
- Can you take a look at...?

**感谢配合：**
- I really appreciate your help.
- Thank you for supporting me.
- You're a great team player.
- Your assistance was invaluable.
- I couldn't have done it without you.

---

### 5.3 处理冲突

**表达关切：**
- I'm a bit concerned about...
- I wanted to bring up...
- I think we have a misunderstanding.
- Can we discuss something?
- I'd like to talk about...
- I feel like we should address...

**解决分歧：**
- Let's find a solution together.
- How can we resolve this?
- I think we can work it out.
- Let's hear both perspectives.
- Perhaps we can compromise.
- I value your input on this.

**道歉与修复：**
- I apologize if I upset you.
- That wasn't my intention.
- I understand your frustration.
- Let's move forward positively.
- I appreciate your patience.

---

### 5.4 赞美与表扬

**赞美同事：**
- Great job on the presentation!
- You did an amazing job!
- Impressive work!
- You're a natural at this!
- The report looks fantastic!
- Kudos to you!

**团队表扬：**
- The team did an excellent job!
- Hats off to everyone involved!
- This was a real team effort!
- You all went above and beyond!
- Special thanks to...

## 六、出差与旅行

### 6.1 预订与安排

**预订表达：**
- I'd like to book a flight to...
- Can you help me with hotel reservations?
- I'll need a rental car.
- Please book the conference room.
- Can we arrange transportation?
- I'd like to extend my stay.

**行程确认：**
- Can you confirm my booking?
- What's the check-in time?
- Where is the meeting location?
- What's the dress code?
- Could you send me the itinerary?
- I'll need travel expenses approved.

---

### 6.2 机场与酒店

**机场表达：**
- I'd like to check in, please.
- Where is the departure gate?
- Is my flight on time?
- I've lost my luggage.
- Can I get a boarding pass?
- Where is the baggage claim?

**酒店表达：**
- I have a reservation under...
- What time is checkout?
- Could I have a wake-up call?
- The room needs housekeeping.
- Is there WiFi available?
- Could you call a taxi for me?

---

### 6.3 会议与活动

**会议参与：**
- I'm attending the conference.
- Where is the registration desk?
- Could you point me to...?
- What's the agenda for today?
- Is lunch provided?
- Where can I network?

**社交活动：**
- Would you like to join us for dinner?
- The networking event starts at 6pm.
- Can I get your business card?
- Let's stay in touch!
- It was great meeting you!

---

### 6.4 商务用餐

**订座表达：**
- I'd like to make a reservation.
- Table for two, please.
- Could we have a table by the window?
- What time does the restaurant close?
- Do you accept credit cards?
- Can we have the bill, please?

**用餐表达：**
- What do you recommend?
- I'll have the same as them.
- This looks delicious!
- Thank you for lunch!
- Let me buy this one.
- Cheers!
